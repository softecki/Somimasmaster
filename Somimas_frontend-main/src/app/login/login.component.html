SOMIMAS is the core banking system for running microfinance institutions. Built by Softecki, it gives SACCOs, micro-lenders, and community banks a single secure platform to manage their entire operation — from client onboarding to loans, savings, accounting, and reporting.

Clients & Groups

Register and manage clients, groups, and centers with complete records, documents, and KYC details.

Loan Management

Handle the full loan cycle: product setup, application, approval, disbursement, repayments, and arrears.

Savings & Deposits

Run savings accounts, fixed and recurring deposits, and share accounts with automated interest posting.

Accounting & Reports

Full double-entry accounting with a chart of accounts, portfolio monitoring, and detailed reports.
