Run your microfinance institution on SOMIMAS

SOMIMAS is the Softecki Microfinance Management System — the core banking platform for SACCOs, micro-lenders, and community banks. Register your organization once, and SOMIMAS provisions a dedicated tenant database, creates your administrator account, and lets your team manage clients, loans, savings, and accounting from a single secure workspace.

Organization onboarding

Register your institution, receive a unique tenant slug, and get a dedicated Fineract database automatically.

Clients & Groups

Register and manage clients, groups, and centers with complete records, documents, and KYC details.

Loan Management

Handle the full loan cycle: product setup, application, approval, disbursement, repayments, and arrears.

Savings & Accounting

Run savings and deposits with double-entry accounting, portfolio monitoring, and operational reports.
